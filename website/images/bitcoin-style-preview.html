BT2C Bitcoin-Style Logo Variations

Version 1

Classic Bitcoin-style with the orange/gold circular background and a tilted "B" symbol. The "2" is integrated into the lower portion of the B.

Version 2

Similar Bitcoin-style with a more prominent "2" in the center of the design. Includes a small "C" to complete the BT2C name.

Version 3

Most Bitcoin-like appearance with the "2" incorporated as negative space within the "B" symbol. Maintains the iconic tilted angle of the Bitcoin logo.
