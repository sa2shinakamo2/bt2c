BT2C (Bit2Coin)

A decentralized digital store of value, built on Bitcoin's proven principles

Store of Value

  • Fixed maximum supply of 21,000,000 BT2C
  • Halving mechanism to ensure scarcity
  • Built for long-term value preservation
  • Resistant to inflation and market manipulation

Pure Validation

  • Focused on secure transaction validation
  • No governance or proposal voting
  • Stake-weighted validator selection
  • Decentralized network of validators
